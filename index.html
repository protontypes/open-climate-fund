About OpenClimate.fund

OpenClimate.fund is an initiative by OpenSustain.tech and Ecosyste.ms to support open source climate and sustainability projects. Open source projects are powering thousands of solutions in the climate and sustainability space. From tracking forest health to designing eco-friendly products, these tools help us build a more sustainable future. The best part on open source is that everyone can see how they work and help make them better.

Many brilliant developers are creating these tools in their spare time. We believe it's time to support their work and help these projects grow. That's why we're building a new way to fund open source climate and sustainability projects.

OpenClimate.fund connects climate-focused open source projects with funding. It's simple: you help us discover great projects, we help fund their development.
